Rizal's Enduring Legacy

Jose Rizal's death became a turning point in Philippine history. His ideas on freedom, equality, and justice laid the groundwork for the eventual independence of the Philippines. Today, Rizal is celebrated as a national hero, with monuments, schools, and institutions named in his honor.

His works continue to be taught in schools and universities worldwide, and his legacy lives on through the values of education, nationalism, and love for one's country.
